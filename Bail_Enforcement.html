    Fugitive Apprehension & Bail Enforcement
    Services in Washington State

    Each year, thousands of people are on the run after skipping their court hearing while out on bail. Many of them are facing serious charges, such as rape, murder or robbery. Our job is to then track down the fugitives and make an arrest. They will then be transported to the County jail where the crime is pending. Our team consist of investigators, former military and active military personnel, who are highly trained in dealing with fugitives. We cover the entire state of Washington with our main locations of services consisting of King County, Pierce County, Mason County, Thurston County, Lewis County, Cowlitz County, and Clark County
